# angular2-tabs usage

```html
// with crossfade
<tabs [crossFade]=true>
  <tab tabTitle="Tab Title 1 ">
    Here is the content of Tab title 1
  </tab>
  <tab tabTitle="Tab Title 2">
    Here is the content of Tab title 2
  </tab>
</tabs>`

// without crossfade
<tabs>
  <tab tabTitle="Tab Title 1 ">
    Here is the content of Tab title 1
  </tab>
  <tab tabTitle="Tab Title 2">
    Here is the content of Tab title 2
  </tab>
</tabs>`
```
